*** Description ***

- This hook allows the target box to be removed in flight by pressing CTRL+T. 
- You can also use the config to turn it off (only applies to XVT/BOP/XWA).

Can be used with X-wing, TIE Fighter, X-Wing vs TIE Fighter, Balance of Power and X-Wing Alliance.

*** Requirements ***

This dll requires:
- Windows XP SP2 or superior
- xw_series_hook_main


*** Install ***

Place hook_targetboxtoggle.dll in the folder with the game exe. Ex. Balance of Power would be in the XvT\BalanceOfPower\z_xvt__.exe
